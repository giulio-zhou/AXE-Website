What is AXΣ?

ΑΧΣ is a national professional chemistry fraternity with nearly 100 chapters that has been at Cal since 1913. We are co-ed and unaffiliated with the Greek system; instead, we are simply a group of people bonded (ha!) by our love of chemistry. As a group, we seek to create a positive social forum for our members, to promote chemistry through academics, service, and professionalism, and to assist our members in succeeding in their professional goals.

Pledging

Pledging is a semester-long process in which students prepare to become members of Alpha Chi Sigma. Anyone with an interest in chemistry can pledge, though pledges must have taken or plan to take three semesters of chemistry coursework.

As a co-ed fraternity, we welcome everyone with a genuine interest in chemistry to apply! We accept members of all majors, ages, and genders.
