Practice Exams

As one of the oldest extant chemistry societies at Berkeley, we've amassed quite a stockpile of past chemistry and chemical engineering exams.

These exams were collected solely to aid students in studying for their exams, and are available to the public solely for such a purpose.

Use the search functionality to narrow down the exams you're looking for (for instance, by course or instructor), and use the table headers to sort by a parameter.

If you are an instructor and would like us to remove any of your exams, please contact webmaster@axe.berkeley.edu.
